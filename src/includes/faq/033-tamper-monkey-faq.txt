Why TamperMonkey?
<p>There are limitations when you don't own the game on Steam resulting in that you can't create any own collection of that game. But you can still subscribe to the mods you want to have for your game and can create with the script the SteamCMD commands from your own workshop page.</p><p>You find your subscriptions within your account. You can copy the following URL and replace the "YOUR-STEAM-USER-NAME" with your account user name: https://steamcommunity.com/id/YOUR-STEAM-USER-NAME/myworkshopfiles?browsefilter=mysubscriptions</p><p>The data will be processed on this webpage. This way I have to care only for a single codebase when there are changes on Steam by Valve.</p>
